Health Tips May 05, 2024

Essential Nutrition Tips for Expecting Mothers

Proper nutrition is key to a healthy pregnancy. Learn about the vital vitamins and minerals you need during each trimester.

During pregnancy, your body needs extra nutrients. We recommend focusing on:

  • Folic Acid: Vital for neural tube development.
  • Iron: To support increased blood volume and prevent anemia.
  • Calcium & Vitamin D: For baby's bone development.

Please consult our nutritionists for a personalized meal plan tailored to your health needs.
